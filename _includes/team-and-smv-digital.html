Meet the team

Our team consists of enthusiastic, flexible and engaged people with a varied set of backgrounds. We work very deliberately to be flexible and efficient, a company full of people who make a positive difference in whichever setting they are placed.

When hiring, we look for people who care about making a difference in their day-to-day life, and as a customer you're sure to experience this, too.

Finding incredible people means thinking hard about the kind of work environment we create. We are always reevaluating, and figuring out how to create an efficient, inclusive and open space to work in, and we bring that spirit with us wherever we work.

Approved advisors

Several of our employees are approved advisors in the SMV:Digital advisor registry.

That means that you may be able to get some of the work on digitalization of your company subsidized while working with us. Companies with 3-249 full-time employees can get 100.000-250.000 DKK in subsidies, and companies with 2-49 full-time employees can get 25.000 DKK if they qualify for the funds.

You can see if you are eligible, and contact us to hear more.
