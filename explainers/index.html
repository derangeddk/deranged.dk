Avoid common mistakes
and build better software

Our explainers are a collection of resources for decision makers in any kind of company, faced with the challenge of building software tools or products to support their business and its needs.

Software is a complex endeavour, and there are many pitfalls for people who are not new to the industry. Hopefully, our explainers will help guide you through some of these, and help you build better tools and products for your employees and customers.

Get more value for money with small deliveries

When building software, it is better to take it into use a bit at a time. That way, you will get more total value for your business. Not only that, you probably also get better software, because small deliveries encourage feedback. Read more.
