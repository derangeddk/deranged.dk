Get more value for money with small deliveries

When building software, it is better to take it into use a bit at a time. That way, you will get more total value for your business. And there are several other reasons why this is a good idea.

In this quick explainer, we will be covering the mechanics at play when delivering software, and the differences between delivering in small increments and delivering in one big delivery at the end of a project.
