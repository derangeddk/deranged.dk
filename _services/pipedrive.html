Why Choose Pipedrive

Pipedrive is a simple to use CRM (Customer relationship management) tool that packs all the tools necessary to get your sales pipeline off the ground and growing your business.

With pipedrive you can manage your customer relationships during the entire sales cycle, from outbound & inbound marketing to closing the deal.

Combined with intelligent automations and a built in AI Sales Assistant, Pipedrive is your go to tool for managing sales leads and deals.

Key Pipedrive features

Automated workflows - These allow you to save time on manual processes and focus on what’s important. Selling more.

Custom fields - Add the data that reflects your sales cycle to your leads and deals.

Email templates - Helps you maximise efficiency by streamlining emails.

Insights - Create custom reports and dashboards so you can see how far you've come, and how far you've got left to go.

Marketing campaigns - Pipedrive's Campaigns add-on enables you to effortlessly create, send, and track email campaigns directly from your CRM. Centralize your sales and marketing in one place with Pipedrive.

Mobile app - This allows your sales team to work on the go - useful for conferences and industry events - without any extra costs to your business

Smart Docs Add-on - Make light work of quotes, proposals and contracts.

Unlimited sales pipelines - If your organisation has multiple sales processes, Pipedrive lets you manage them with ease.
