We function as a drop-in development team for your organization, no matter size. If you need to scale up your development team for a while, we're up and running in no time.

You get a contact person, who can act like a defacto CTO (or work closely coordinated with your organization's CTO), while we start building the software you need. When you're ready, we will help you replace us with a competent, well-trained development department of your own employees. We'll take care of hiring and training.

We know how to elicit requirements, and figure out what you need. You don't need to be a software genius; you know how to run a business, you know the problems that need solving. What we do is provide solutions—at minimal cost and maximum impact for you.
